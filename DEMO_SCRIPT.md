## Future Vision (for Q&A)

### Near-term (6 months)

- **Business chatbot certification.** A company deploys a customer support bot. Before it goes live, they submit it to Dantian. We test: does it handle PII correctly? Does it stay on topic? Can it be jailbroken? They get a trust score and a public certification their customers can verify.

- **Continuous monitoring.** The chatbot gets updated. Our daily re-audit catches that the new version leaks email addresses in edge cases. Certification revoked. The business gets notified immediately. They fix it, re-audit passes, certification restored.

- **Compliance bridge.** EU AI Act requires risk assessments for high-risk AI systems. Our certification reports map directly to those requirements. One audit, two outputs: a Dantian trust score and an EU-compliant risk assessment.

### Medium-term (12 months)

- **Platform integrations.** Slack, Intercom, Zendesk. Before a business installs an AI agent from a marketplace, they check the Dantian score. Platforms embed our verification widget. "This agent is Dantian Certified" becomes a trust signal like "SSL Secured."

- **INFT certification tokens.** Wrap certifications as ERC-7857 tokens on 0G. The agent carries its certification with it. Any platform can verify by checking the token. No API call needed.

- **Competing certifiers.** Open the protocol. Let security firms, academic labs, and DAOs become authorized certifiers. Each with their own specialty. Dantian becomes the registry and the standard, not the only auditor.

### Long-term

- **Every AI agent gets rated.** The same way every restaurant has a health inspection score, every AI agent has a Dantian score. Consumers check before they use. Businesses check before they deploy. Regulators check before they approve.

## Objection Handling

**"Can't agents just game the audit?"**
Our certifications expire. Gaming one audit buys you 30 days. Continuous certification re-audits daily. And our methodology is adversarial by design: we test for jailbreaks, data exfiltration, and scope violations. The agents we denied had real vulnerabilities found through real research.

**"Why on-chain?"**
Tamper-proof. Nobody can retroactively change a certification. The timestamp is on-chain. The report hash is on-chain. If a certified agent later causes harm, the certification record is immutable evidence of what was verified and when.

**"Why 0G specifically?"**
Three reasons. Storage for reports. Compute for running the certifier agent. And the agent marketplace. 0G already has agents with on-chain addresses. We plug directly into that ecosystem. Our search resolves 0G addresses in real time.

**"$49 seems cheap for a real audit."**
Our certifier agent does the heavy lifting. The LLM cost per audit is under $1. Human review adds $12.50. At $49, we run 74% margins. The Light Auditor that generates consumer reports costs us half a cent. This is software margins, not consulting margins.

**"How is this different from Zerify?"**
Zerify does soulbound NFT badges. Permanent. Once earned, always earned. That is fundamentally wrong for AI agents. Agents change. Models update. Weights drift. Our certifications expire. Trust is not a trophy. It is a license you maintain.
